I have no clue what is this GPG thing about!

Well, then you probably don't need it :) But anyway you can read something about it on Wikipedia.

Is this based in US?

No, it's in Czech Republic, EU.

So is this super secure kind of email?

Absolutely not. The email can be intercepted on the way to your alias. And there are other constraints. But your Xmail provider will not be able to read your emails, that's for sure.

I have forgotten my password - what can I do?

Sorry, there is no possibility to retrieve lost password.

Can I view my current GPG key?

No, you can just change it.

Do I have to use Mailvelope?

Absolutelly not. It's just most easy and comfortable way in my opinion. You can use Gpg4win or WebPG or anything else which is GPG capable.

So this project is based on opensource right?

Yes, it is built on Ubuntu, Postfix, Dovecot, GnuPG, Apache, Bootstrap, MySQL, gpgit and many others.
